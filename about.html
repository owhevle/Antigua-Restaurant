Landmark

Antigua Restaurant, also known as Cafe Antigua, is one of the oldest dining spots in Lucena City, Quezon Province. It has built a reputation over the years for serving delicious traditional Filipino dishes.

Gatherings

Cafe Antigua had wide function rooms in their upper floors which served as venues for many weddings. It was also perfect for hosting memorable events.
